Privacy Policy – Learning Cards

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Learning Cards, we collect the following information:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • User-generated content (cards, lessons, and images you create)
  • Cards shared between users via the inbox feature
  • Profile information (display name, profile photo if provided)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide account functionality (login, registration, password reset)
  • To store your cards and lessons on your device
  • To enable card sharing between users
  • To process in-app subscription purchases

3. Data Storage

Cards and lessons are stored on your device only (local storage) by default. Only account information, card sharing data, and premium subscription status are sent to Supabase servers.

Data sent to Supabase is securely stored with SSL/TLS encryption. Images uploaded to cards and shared with others are stored in Supabase Storage.

4. In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional annual premium subscription processed via Google Play Billing. We store your subscription status but do not store any payment information. All payments are handled entirely by Google Play.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Card sharing only occurs between users you explicitly choose to send cards to.

6. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated server data at any time via Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will delete your email, profile information, shared cards, and premium status. Local cards and lessons on your device are automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Learning Cards is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
