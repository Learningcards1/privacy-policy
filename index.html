Privacy Policy

Section 01

Data We Collect

When you use Learning Cards, we may collect the following categories of information:

Data Type Purpose Stored Where
Email address Account creation, login, password reset Supabase servers
Display name & profile photo User profile (optional) Supabase servers
Cards & lessons Core app functionality Device only (local)
Shared card content Card sharing between users Supabase servers (temporary)
Images uploaded to cards Card media Supabase Storage

📷 Camera & Photo Library: The app may request access to your camera and photo library to let you add images to cards. This access is used solely to attach images you choose — we do not access your photos without your explicit action.
Section 02

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To sync your profile and subscription status across devices
  • To enable the card sharing (inbox) feature between users
  • To process and verify in-app subscription purchases via Google Play
  • To respond to account deletion requests and support inquiries

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

Section 03

Data Storage & Security

Cards and lessons are stored on your device only by default. Only the data listed in Section 1 as "Supabase servers" leaves your device.

All data transmitted to Supabase is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS. Supabase uses Row Level Security (RLS) policies so that each user can only access their own data.

Data retention: Account data is retained until you delete your account. Shared card data sent to another user's inbox is retained until that user accepts or dismisses it. Images are deleted from Supabase Storage when the associated card or account is deleted.

Section 04

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional annual Premium subscription ($4.99/year) processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not store any payment card or billing information. We only store your subscription status and expiry date to unlock premium features.

Subscription management and cancellation can be done directly from the Google Play app under Subscriptions.

Section 05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third parties.

The only sharing that occurs:

  • Card sharing: When you explicitly send a card to another user by their email address, that card content is transmitted to their inbox. The recipient can see your display name and email.
  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider. Data is processed per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play: Subscription status is verified via Google Play. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
Section 06

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.

To delete your account and all server data: Go to Settings → Delete Account inside the app. This is permanent and irreversible — it deletes your email, profile, shared cards, uploaded images, and subscription status from our servers.

To delete local data: Uninstall the app. All locally stored cards and lessons are removed automatically.

🇪🇺 GDPR (EU users): You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address in Section 9.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

Learning Cards is a general-purpose educational app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

⚠️ If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately so we can delete their data.

The app includes user-generated content and person-to-person card sharing. If used by minors aged 13–17, parental guidance is recommended when interacting with the sharing features.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users (for example, via an in-app notice). Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
